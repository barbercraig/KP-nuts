How to turn on cookies in Microsoft Internet Explorer 6, Internet Explorer 7 and Internet Explorer 8:

  1. In the browser, choose Tools
  2. Select Internet Options
  3. Click the Privacy tab
  4. Adjust the 'Settings' slider to a lower level

How to turn on cookies in Mozilla Firefox 3:

  1. In the browser, choose Tools and select Options
  2. Click the Privacy tab
  3. Check the box labeled 'Accept cookies from sites'
  4. Click OK

How to turn on cookies in Netscape browsers:

  1. From the Edit menu, select Preferences
  2. Click Advanced
  3. Click to select the box labeled Accept All Cookies
  4. Click OK

How to turn on cookies in Safari:

  1. From the Safari menu (Mac) or Edit menu (Windows), click Preferences
  2. From the Security dialog box, from Accept Cookies, click to select Always
  3. Click Close

How to turn on cookies in Google Chrome

  1. Click the icon
  2. Click Options
  3. Select the 'Under the Hood' tab
  4. Under 'Cookie Settings' select 'Allow all cookies' from the drop-down
  5. Click 'Close'
