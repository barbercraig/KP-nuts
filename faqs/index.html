Who is eligible?
Open to residents of the UK, IoM and ROI (excludes Channel Island residents). 16+ only. Under 18s require parent/guardian’s consent. Excludes employees and their immediate families of United Biscuits (UK) Limited, its agents and anyone professionally connected with this promotion.

How do I enter?
Purchase a pack of KP Nuts.
Either SMS KP followed by a space, Batchcode followed by a space then the players name for example: KP 9225MC8A BANKS. If you are having problems finding the codes this is located under the 'best before' area on the back of the pack.
Or visit www.kpnuts.com, select your favourite from the 10 best quality football moments and follow the process to submit your vote. You must then enter your email address, full name and house name or number, including post code and follow the instructions (ROI entrants will be required to enter their street name and town instead of a postcode).

The Grand Prize Draw can be entered separately online without prior entry into the instant win promotion. No purchase necessary for entry into the Grand Prize Draw if entering separately via the website. Only one entry per person into the Grand Prize Draw regardless of the route of entry.

What is a batch code?
This is a code of between 7 and 9 digits on the back of a KP Nuts pack. The batch code is situated on the bottom left hand corner below the Best Before date eg: 9225 MC8A.

Where can I buy a pack?
Send in your name, house number, address and full valid post code along with your pack to: KP Nuts ‘Vote for the best quality football moment’ PO Box 3, Diss, Norfolk, IP98 1HH, UK and a member of the KP Nuts team will enter on your behalf. Only one entry will be accepted per person.

How do I win?
Follow the entry process (see ‘How do I enter’). Each day's online prizes have been pre-allocated and linked to “winning moments", which have been randomly pre-selected via a secure, independently verified program. If your entry is at or the next nearest to one of these times, you will be a winner.

Must I purchase a promotional pack to enter?
Yes, you do a need promotional pack to enter. (Excludes NI residents).

I live in Northern Ireland and would like to enter via the no purchase necessary

Your entry will be entered into the entry system at a random time within one week after receipt. Only one entry per properly stamped envelope. Winners only will be notified via post within 28 days. Non-NI entries will be disqualified.

What are the prizes?
Instant win prize fund: Mini Footballs, Mini Table Footballs, Replica Shirts. The replica shirts will be awarded in the form of a voucher code which can be redeemed online at: www.subsidesports.com. Voucher codes are valid until August 2011. All prizes will be awarded.

Grand Draw: 1 x Football Trip for 2 people/adults to a European destination of your choice from 6 pre-selected destinations, 2 nights accommodation at a 4* hotel, tickets to see a domestic league match and a total of £200/€250 spending money. Trip includes return flights, accommodation, airport transfers, daily breakfast, transfers to the stadium and tickets to see a domestic league match and spending money. Insurance is not included; winners are responsible for their own visas, passports and meeting entry requirements. Operator Terms & Conditions apply.

When is the closing date for the Grand Prize draw?
The Grand prize draw closing date is midnight 30th September 2010 for the Instant Win and 31st December 2010 for the Grand Draw. Winners of the final draws will be notified within 28 days of the closing dates.

How many times can I enter?
In order to provide a fair promotion for as many consumers as possible the following restrictions will be rigidly applied:

UK and ROI online entries: 5 entries per person per day. The same combination of batchcode can be entered 5 times across the whole promotion by a person. No entrant can use their computer to enable multiple entries. See full promotion terms and conditions for more details.

How will I know if I'm a winner?
All winners will be informed instantly online or via SMS if they have won. If any winner cannot be contacted within 28 days from first attempting to do so, or after initial contact does not send in the appropriate details for validation, or does not meet the verification criteria then the promoter reserves the right to withdraw prize entitlement and re-draw their prize.

How can I claim my prize?
SMS entrants will be contacted on the mobile phone number used to enter with instructions on how to claim.

All prize winners must respond to winner notification within 28 days by sending in their winning pack, with legible batch code, along with their name and full postal address including postcode in block capitals on a plain piece of paper to Mad About Handling, c/o KP Nuts ‘Vote for the best quality football moment’, 18 & 19 Burway Trading Estate, Bromfield Road, Ludlow, Shropshire SY8 1EN

How many times can I win?
Instant Win: One prize per random winning moment. Maximum of 3 wins per person over the duration of the promotion.

Consumers who try to claim a prize by sending in a pack for verification that has a different batchcode to that submitted at time of entry will have their major prize claim disqualified. If you are in possession of multiple packs that contain the same batchcode combination contact the consumer services line for more information.

If we are not experiencing any problems with the website and you have a correct code, we would advise you to send your pack to our handling house who will enter on your behalf.

Only one entry will be accepted per person.

The postcode I entered online is different to the address I would like the prize to be sent to. Can I still claim my prize?
For security reasons we will only send prizes to the name and address entered online. Any photocopy of a utility bill, council tax bill, mobile phone bill or drivers license sent in as part of prize verification process must clearly show the same name and address that were submitted as part of the online promotion entry.

I have been told that my prize claim is invalid. Why is this?
Any of the following will result in an invalid prize claim:

  • Your name does not match across the web entry and the claim documents.
  • Your address does not match across the web entry and the claim documents.
  • The batch code you entered on the website does not match the batch code on the pack you sent in.
  • There was no pack supplied with your claim pack.
  • The pack you sent in was damaged, defaced, copied or defective.
  • Your claim pack did not contain the necessary requirements.
  • Your claim was received 28 days after the close date of 31.08.2010.
  • You have exceeded the maximum entries permitted.
  • The required age limit is 16 and is open only to residents of the UK, Isle of Mann and the Republic of Ireland. Excludes Channel Islands residents.
  • You have previously been awarded the maximum number of prizes.

Prizes will only be dispatched upon verification of identity and packs. All prizes will only be dispatched to the full address provided at time of registration. If any winner cannot be contacted after reasonable efforts within 28 days from first attempting to do so, or after initial contact does not send in the appropriate details for verification within 28 days, or does not meet the verification criteria then the promoter reserves the right to disqualify the entrant and withdraw the prize entitlement.

Should the Promoter have any reason to believe that there has been a breach of the Terms and Conditions, or the spirit of the Promotion, the Promoter may at its sole discretion refuse to process a win claim. The Promoter will be the final arbiter in any decisions and these will be final and binding. No correspondence will be entered into.

The Promoter reserves the right in its sole discretion to disqualify any person who in their opinion corrupts or affects the proper conduct of the promotion, attempts to damage the website or otherwise undermine the legitimate operation of the promotion. If such activity is suspected the Promoter will delete the offending entries and compensation or prosecution will be sought through the law.

Any entrant attempting to exceed their daily entrance allowance either manually or by using their computer to enable multiple entries will be disqualified and any prize award will be void. For instance this includes any attempts made by an entrant to change their contact details in order to exceed more than the allowable 5 entries per person per day will result in disqualification.
